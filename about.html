Russell Lyon

GIS Analyst & Developer

I’ve been using maps to guide decision-makers for over a decade mostly in the environmental and natural resource sectors. For most of my career I have relied heavily on ESRI products, but for the last 3 years I have begun to use more open-source programs including QGIS and GRASS. Lately, I’ve also been focusing a lot more on web mapping using OSM data, Leaflet JS Libraries and Mapbox Tiles.

The Kay Pacha page is an example that still needs a lot of work, but I enjoy learning and working on it in my spare time. Thanks to Codecademy I have a working knowledge of JavaScript, Python and HTML/CSS but I feel like there’s always more for me to learn. I’m better at borrowing code from open sources and tailoring it for my needs. This portfolio and the Kay Pacha page are hosted on Github and in setting them up I have learned most of the Git commands and how to create and merge branches.

Taking advantage of my Navy’s ESRI subscription I get access to all of the training courses and some recent ones I’ve taken include Map Automation with Python, LiDAR in ArcGIS 10 and Getting Started with Linear Referencing. I run mainly QGIS at home and I’ve found that with all the plug-ins available (SAGA, GDAL, etc.), I can perform the same tasks as in ArcMap.

I’m happy that I found a career that I truly enjoy. My current contract position with the Navy focuses more on utility data editing, which is not bad, but I would much rather return to working in Natural Resource Management. I took this job because it was an easy way to transition back to working in the US after my year in Peru, but I feel I’m not really being challenged and am ready to move on.

Apart from GIS and my professional life I enjoy travelling, backpacking, biking and disc golf. I am fluent in Spanish thanks to the three years I lived in South America and I even picked up some Quechua when I lived in Bolivia. I love Andean culture and try to make it to Peru or Bolivia at least once a year. I work well alone or as part of a team and am always respectful towards others and am easy to get along with.

So that’s me in a nutshell.
